## General

This website processes only the data strictly necessary for operation.
There are **no cookies**, **no tracking**, **no analytics**, and no external resources.

## Server logs

When visiting this website, technical data is stored in server logs:

- IP address (anonymised after 7 days)
- date and time of access
- requested URL
- browser identification (User-Agent)
- referrer

This data is used exclusively for **technical troubleshooting** and **attack defence**
(legal basis: Art. 6 (1) lit. f GDPR — legitimate interest). It is automatically
deleted after **30 days**.

## No cookies, no tracking

This website does **not use cookies** and **no tracking** mechanisms. No data is
transmitted to third parties.

The theme preference (light/dark) is stored only in the browser's `localStorage` —
never on the server.

## Your rights

You have the right at any time to:

- **Access** (Art. 15 GDPR)
- **Rectification** (Art. 16 GDPR)
- **Erasure** (Art. 17 GDPR)
- **Restriction** (Art. 18 GDPR)
- **Object** (Art. 21 GDPR)
- **Complaint** to a supervisory authority (Art. 77 GDPR)
