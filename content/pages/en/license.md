## LaTeX Project Public License 1.3c

KOMA-Script is released under the **LaTeX Project Public License (LPPL) Version 1.3c**.
The LPPL is a **free software license** designed specifically for LaTeX packages.

## Key points

### You may

✅ **Use** KOMA-Script freely, privately, in research and commercially.
✅ **Redistribute** it, standalone or as part of a collection.
✅ **Modify** it, provided you comply with the LPPL terms.

### Note

⚠️ **Modifications** must be distributed under a **different file name**, so users
can identify whether they have the original or a modified version.

⚠️ The **license file** must be included on redistribution.

⚠️ **Author information** must be preserved.
