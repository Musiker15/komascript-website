## Help & Discussion

### TeX Stack Exchange

The fastest way to get help is
[TeX Stack Exchange](https://tex.stackexchange.com/questions/tagged/koma-script) under
the **`koma-script`** tag. Thousands of questions are already answered — and Markus Kohm
himself frequently replies.

## Social

- **Mastodon:** [@koma@mastodontech.de](https://mastodontech.de/@koma)
- **GitHub:** [komascript](https://github.com/komascript)

## Bug reports

Report bugs via the SourceForge tracker:
[Bug Tracker](https://sourceforge.net/p/koma-script/_list/tickets)

Spin-off projects use their own trackers on GitHub or Codeberg.

<Callout type="tip">
Before filing a bug: create a **minimal working example** (MWE) and provide the
exact **version number** (`\listfiles`).
</Callout>
