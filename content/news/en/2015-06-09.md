I congratulate [Dr. Ingeborg
Syllm-Rapoport](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ingeborg_Rapoport) on her [newly
earned
doctorate](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ingeborg_Rapoport#Life_in_retirement_and_Doctorate). That
was a courageous and admirable step. Especially in light of her own
explanation of why she decided to take this step, I can only say: “Hats off!”
Giving the victims—not the criminals of that time—the last word deserves our
attention.
