The new blog has been live here on “KOMA-Script and Friends” since
yesterday. It all started when I came across an interesting post while
cleaning up the old blog and decided to find a new home for it. The solution
was to launch the blog section of
[hugo-book](https://themes.gohugo.io/themes/hugo-book/).

Since the previous blog in the “KOMA-Script Documentation Project” was
published exclusively in German, I won’t be translating any old posts—with
very few exceptions, such as the one mentioned above. New posts will also
appear in English only in exceptional cases.

Sorry!  
*Markus*
